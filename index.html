Eco-Engineering Meets Advanced IoT

We combine raw local materials with smart engineering to deliver maximum energy efficiency, modern safety, and transparent asset management.

Bioclimatic Insulation

Constructed using local Compressed Stabilized Earth Bricks (CSEB). Lowers construction grey energy by 60% and drops indoor temperatures by 4°C, reducing AC load.

Proprietary IoT Kit

Low-cost ESP32 microcontrollers assembled locally and paired with a Django gateway. Automatically manages power loads and shuts down water on leakage alarm.

Diaspora Gateway

Transparent digital management portal. Allows African diaspora to easily co-invest in local developments and track rent cash flows securely via Mobile Money.
